## ⚖️ Rulings of The Supreme Administrative Court

[The Supreme Administrative Court of the Czech Republic](https://www.nssoud.cz/) (Nejvyšší správní soud České republiky) is the court of highest authority on issues of judicial review of executive (and regulatory) action. It also has jurisdiction over competence disputes and many political matters, such as the elections, the formation and closure of political parties, the eligibility of persons to stand for public office, etc. It also adjudicates in disciplinary proceedings against judges and state prosecutors.

The Court publishes its rulling on their website, from where we scrape them and put these documents on-chain.


### View the on-chain rulings

You can view the rulings on any Cardano blockchain explorer that supports the metadata, they are published as proof-of-concept [under the 1234 metadata label](https://preview.cexplorer.io/metadata/1234).

The brige between the Court and Denotarius is also open-source and is published at [denotarius-registry](https://github.com/denotarius/denotarius-registry).
